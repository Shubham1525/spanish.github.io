ABOUT US



About Mafalda in Spanish



iBienvenidos! Soy Karina Welcome to Mafalda in Spanish, I am Karina and I have been teaching Spanish in Secondary Schools in England for the last 15 years. Originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina I combine language lessons with cultural knowledge of both my cherished Latin America and Spain. .




Who is Mafalda?



Mafalda is an Argentine cartoon close to my heart and my childhood. In brief, she is a little girl wise beyond her years who lives in a middle-class family in Buenos Aires. She is known for her curiosity, political and social views, and always worrying about humanity and world peace.





Why Mafalda?



Mafalda offers a tutoring service based in Tring, Hertfordshire but also provides quality Spanish tuition elsewhere both face to face and online. I designed lessons for all age groups to suit my students’ needs, varying from beginners, conversational Spanish or preparing students for GCSE & A-level exams as well as the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages A1 to C2). I offer both individual lessons and group ones too. I will be delighted to be sharing my love for language learning and the culture of the second most spoken language worldwide with you. iHasta pronto!


Karina Simón













OUR STUDENTS

Originally, our students came from Battersea and Clapham, but as the numbers grow so does our coverage, and we are happy to welcome students from all of South West London: all of Wandsworth, Putney, Vauxhall, Brixton, Pimlico, Fulham, Chelsea, South Kensington and even as far as Islington, Angel and Shoreditch. We welcome anyone looking for a different and rewarding Latin/Spanish experience!



Native speakers have joined our centre through the cultural events we hold, and courses such as the Creative Writing Workshops in Spanish in Poetry and Narrative, as well as the Dance courses.
