About Mafalda in Spanish



Welcome to Mafalda in Spanish, I am Karina and I have been teaching Spanish in Secondary Schools in England for the last 15 years. Originally from Buenos Aires, Argentina, I combine language lessons with cultural knowledge of both my cherished Latin america and Spain.

Mafalda is an argentine cartoon character close to my heart and my childhood. In brief, she is a little girl wise beyond her years who lives in a middle-class family in Buenos Aires. She is known for her curiosity, political and social views, and is always worrying about humanity and world peace.

Mafalda offers a tutoring service based in Tring, Hertfordshire, but also provides quality Spanish tuition elsewhere both face to face and online.

I designed lessons for all age groups to suit my students’ needs, varying from beginners, conversational Spanish or preparing students for GCSE & A-level exams as well as the CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages A1 to C2). I offer both individual lessons and group ones too.
I will be delighted to be sharing my love for language learning and the culture of the second most spoken language worldwide with you.
iHasta pronto!

Karina Simón
